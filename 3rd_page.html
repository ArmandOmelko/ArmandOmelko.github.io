My first topic is technological. This topic fits the subject because Boeing is an aircraft designer and manufacturer which means the deal with technology.
Boeing built many important planes. These planes such as the 707, 737, and 747 left a technological impact on Washington.
The 707, for example, advanced jet technology substantialy, and made jet technology more common in Washington.
Another example is the 737, this is the best selling plane ever and made regional jet planes more common, this affected Washington technologicaly as it made regional jets,
a new technology more common in the state. Finally, the 747. This plane was the first wide-body plane in the world meaning it was a new technology.
The 747 also required a new factory to be built in order for it to fit inside.
These show that Boeing left an impact on Washington because they show what Boeing built and what new things they had or that had come along with them.
